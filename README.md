# Supply Chain Demand Forecasting & Scenario Simulator

## Business Problem
Supply chain disruptions and demand volatility make point forecasts
insufficient for planning.

## Solution
Built a demand forecasting and scenario simulation framework to help
operations teams prepare for uncertainty.

## Tech Stack
Python, Time Series Forecasting, Operations Analytics

## Key Outcomes
- Improved demand visibility
- Quantified risk under demand shocks
- Enabled proactive inventory planning

## Future Enhancements
- Multi-SKU forecasting
- Seasonality modeling
- Integration with ERP systems
